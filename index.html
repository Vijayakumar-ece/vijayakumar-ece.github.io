About Me

I am Vijayakumar S, an Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Muthayammal Engineering College with a strong interest in embedded systems, microcontroller programming, and intelligent hardware applications.

I enjoy working at the intersection of electronics and software — designing systems that combine sensing, processing, and real-world interaction. My technical foundation includes ARM-based microcontrollers, Arduino, ESP32, and communication protocols like UART, I2C, SPI.

I also have exposure to computer vision and image processing through YOLOv8 and OpenCV, and I apply UI/UX design principles using Figma — having contributed to the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) as a UI/UX designer.

In Jan–Feb 2026, I served as a Research Intern at NITTTR Chennai, working on underwater image processing using YOLO-based detection. My goal is a career in embedded systems and intelligent electronics product development.
